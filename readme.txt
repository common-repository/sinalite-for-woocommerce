== Description ==

With Printbest's WooCommerce plugin for print on demand dropshipping, you have the power to wow your clients more quickly, easily, and profitably. Our product prices are on average 20% lower than leading competitors, so you can save on costs and profit more!

Sell printed on T-shirts, hoodies, tank tops, and more to clients all over the United States and Canada while you relax. Our direct-to-garment printing experts use cutting edge Kornit technology to bring you an unlimited color palette with no order minimums.

= What is Printbest? =
Printbest is a print on demand and dropshipping service that helps you fulfill your online orders. Our WooCommerce plugin provides live shipping rates so that your store can easily match our shipping rates.

= How Printbest Works =
When your customers order from your WooCommerce store products that are synced with Printbest, we're automatically notified. We print and dropship it directly to your customer under your brand. It's a risk-free way to profit!

= What to Expect =
* Lower prices: Enjoy affordable prices without order minimums.
* Easy fulfillment: Say goodbye to inventory. We automatically process the orders you receive.
* Customization options: Create personalized products and see them in our mockup generator.
* Fast production: We finish printing in 1-5 business days and promptly ship orders out.
* Convenient dropshipping: We use a flat rate for anywhere in your country.
* Control over your profit: You set your retail price, we charge you for production, and what's left is your profit.

== Installation ==
1. Upload “Printbest for WooCommerce” to the “/wp-content/plugins/” directory
1. Activate the plugin through the “Plugins” menu in WordPress
1. (optional) Enable shipping rate calculation in WooCommerce >  Settings > Shipping > Printbest Shipping tab
